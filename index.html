Frequently Asked

Common questions

How do I book an appointment?

The fastest way is via WhatsApp at +91 96030 62942. You can also call the clinic directly during OPD hours or walk in.

What should I bring for my first visit?

Please carry your Aadhaar card (or any government ID), all previous prescriptions and lab reports, your current medication list, and any blood sugar / BP records you maintain.

Do you offer online consultations?

Yes. Teleconsultation is available for follow-ups and for patients outside Ongole, in line with India's Telemedicine Practice Guidelines 2020. Contact us on WhatsApp to set one up.

Which languages does Dr. Geeta speak?

Consultations are available in Telugu, English, and Hindi.

Will my diabetes medication change?

Treatment is personalized. Dr. Geeta will review your history and reports carefully before recommending any change — and always explain the reasoning.

How often will I need follow-ups?

It depends on your condition and treatment stage. Most diabetes and thyroid patients are reviewed every 1–3 months initially, then less frequently once stable.
